TipMate Bill Splitter Pro

Privacy Policy

This page explains how TipMate Bill Splitter Pro handles information when you use the app.

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

TipMate Bill Splitter Pro ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to work with minimal data collection.

Information you enter may include:

  • Bill amount
  • Tip percentage
  • Number of people splitting the bill

This information is used only to perform calculations inside the App.

The App may display ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Advertising identifiers
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostics and performance data

2. How We Use Information

The App uses information to:

  • Calculate tips and bill splits
  • Display and measure advertising
  • Maintain app functionality and stability

3. Local Processing

Tip calculations are processed locally on your device. The App does not require account creation and does not ask for your name, email address, or payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services, including Google AdMob for advertising.

These third parties may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob: https://developers.google.com/admob

5. Data Sharing

We do not directly sell your personal information. However, third-party advertising providers may collect, use, and share data as described in their own policies for ad delivery, analytics, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to:

  • Limit ad tracking in device settings
  • Reset your advertising identifier
  • Manage privacy and tracking permissions on your device

8. Data Retention

The App itself does not store personal account information. Any automatically collected data handled by advertising providers is retained according to their own retention policies.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the App secure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Users

If you use the App outside your home country, information collected by third-party services may be transferred to and processed in other countries according to their policies.
